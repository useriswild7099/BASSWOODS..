The Origin

Made from Adventure

Since our first festival expedition in 2019, BASSWOODS has been committed to showcasing the raw, unfiltered beauty of Northeast India. We believe that stepping outdoors shapes us and makes us who we are.

Operating across Meghalaya, Assam, Nagaland, Arunachal, and Mizoram, we provide the logistical backbone for the region's most iconic festivals and wilderness treks.
