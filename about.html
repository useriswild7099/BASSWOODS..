The Origin

Made from adventure

We’re Basswoods Camp, and we’re made from adventure. We believe that stepping outdoors shapes us, moulds us, and makes us who we are. That’s why we specialise in crafting life-affirming camping, touring, and festival experiences across Northeast India.

Basswoods Camp grew from a passion for exploration and a desire to offer end-to-end adventure solutions. The traditional style of travel — predictable itineraries and boring stays — didn’t excite us. We wanted to provide the ultimate blend of thrilling sports, unique campsite stays, and premium car/bike rentals so you can truly unleash the adventurer in you.

Our journey has led us to partner with the region's most iconic events, from the Ziro Festival of Music and Cherry Blossom Festival to the Orange Festival. We don't just set up tents; we curate unforgettable experiences, whether it's an after-party in Cherrapunji or an ATV ride in Shillong.

Today, our footprint spans Meghalaya, Assam, Nagaland, Arunachal Pradesh, and Mizoram. With our expert local teams, premium camping equipment, and a community of passionate explorers, Basswoods Camp is your ultimate travel guide to conquering Northeast India.
