The Contract

Expedition ET&C

1. Physical Integrity & Medical Risk

Participants acknowledge that BASSWOODS expeditions involve deployment into remote wilderness areas where medical facilities are basic and extraction times may vary from 12 to 48 hours. You confirm that you have no pre-existing cardiac or pulmonary conditions that would be exacerbated by altitude (up to 12,000 ft). You are required to disclose all allergies and past surgical history to our Medical Liaison before deployment.

2. Environmental Ethics Bond (LNT Policy)

By booking, you agree to a 'Zero-Litter' mandate. Any found non-biodegradable waste left by the participant will result in immediate termination of the expedition experience without refund. We operate under a strict 'Leave No Trace' covenant. Feeding wildlife or disturbing indigenous archaeological sites is strictly prohibited.

3. Force Majeure & Itinerary Drift

The Northeast Frontier is subject to rapid geological and political changes (landslides, flash-floods, tribal Bandhs, or state-mandated lockdowns). BASSWOODS reserves the right to alter itineraries mid-expedition to ensure squad safety. Refunds are not applicable for weather-induced disruptions. However, credit notes valid for 12 months will be issued for cancellations due to state-enforced travel bans.

4. Cancellation & Refund Logistics

  • - 30+ Days before Start: 80% Refund (20% Retention Fee)
  • - 15-30 Days before Start: 50% Refund
  • - < 15 Days before Start: 0% Refund (Full retention)
  • - Festival Tickets (Hornbill/Ziro/Orange): Non-refundable from the date of booking as per venue policy.
