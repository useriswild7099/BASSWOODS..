Driver's Briefing

Technical Off-Road Log

Northeast terrain is a laboratory of traction variables. Our technical log provides specific handling protocols for the most challenging sectors of the frontier.

SELA PASS: Black Ice

Protocol: Engagement of 4-Low range mandatory. Tire pressure reduction to 25 PSI. Low-torque steady throttle to prevent surface fracture.

CRITICAL: Traction Control OFF on Deep Slush

SIANG RIVER: Boulders

Protocol: Scout wading depth (max 700mm). Diagonally approach submerged boulders. Continuous throttle during wading to prevent tailpipe suction.

CRITICAL: Differential Breather Verification

Fleet Technical Archive

The BASSWOODS Fleet is engineered for the specific mechanical stresses of the Northeast frontier. Volume III focuses on the technical schematics and recovery footprints of our core deployment units.

CLASS: LEGACY 4X4

THAR 2.2L.

Technical Schematic

Our modified Mahindra Thar units (mHawk 130) are the backbone of high-altitude transits. Specifically tuned for low-end torque required in Sela Pass slush and Siang boulders. Each unit is equipped with a 9500lb ComeUp winch and BFGoodrich AT-KO2 tires for maximum traction variables.

RECOVERY KIT

Sand ladders, kinetic snatch straps (10-ton), high-lift jacks, and ARB tire repair kits.

MAINTENANCE LOG

Differential oil swap every 5,000km. Hub greasing post-wading. Air filter blow-out daily.

CLASS: MOTO-ADV

HIMALAYAN.

Technical Schematic

The RE Himalayan 450 cycle is our primary scout vehicle. Featuring Sherpa 450 liquid-cooled engines and long-travel Showa suspension. Light footprint for river cross-transits where larger 4x4s may hang-up. All bikes are fitted with Barkbuster handguards and heavy-duty radiator shielding.

SPARE MODULE

Clutch cables, brake pads, chain-link master, and heavy-duty tubes (even for tubeless).

FUEL DEPOT

15L auxiliary jerry cans per 3-bike squad for Ziro-Mechuka transits.
