The Frontier Code

Leave No Trace

01. WASTE MANAGEMENT

Strict 'Pack It In, Pack It Out' policy. All non-biodegradable waste is tactically removed from wilderness zones within 24 hours of site decommissioning.

02. WATER RECLAMATION

We use specialized bio-filters for greywater management at our permanent hubs (Meghalaya) to preserve local aquifer integrity.

Logistics Intel

Camping Protocol

What are the operational hours?

Check-in: 14:00 HRS. Check-out: 10:00 HRS. Late checkout subject to site tactical availability and peak-season demand.

What is the pet policy?

Pets are welcome at our Ziro and Meghalaya hubs but must remain leashed to protect local wildlife corridors and bypass ecological disruption.

Is medical support available?

All BASSWOODS campsites feature a Level-1 First Aid station. Festival sites have shuttle priority to the nearest multi-specialty regional hub.
