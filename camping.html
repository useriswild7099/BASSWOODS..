FRONTIER PROTOCOL

Technical Stewardship

01 / ECOLOGY

WASTE NEUTRALITY

We operate on a zero-footprint mandate. All non-biodegradable materials are extracted from site daily. Leave nothing but tire tracks in the mud.

02 / IMPACT

SURFACE INTEGRITY

Stick to existing game-trails and established camp boundaries. Preserve the ancient root systems that define the Meghalaya and Ziro plateau.

03 / VIBES

NOISE SILENCE

In the wild, silence is a technical requirement. Respect the auditory space of the locals and the wildlife. Low-decibel operations only.

04 / ENERGY

FIRE DISCIPLINE

Designated pits only. We never harvest living wood. Every ember is neutralized before evacuation to prevent alpine wildfires.
