Things to Do

An Adventure at Every Turn

Basswoods camps aren't just a place to sleep — they're the launchpad for the best experiences Northeast India has to offer.

Trekking
Guided trails through forests, valleys, and tea-garden hillsides. All skill levels welcome.
Fishing & Angling
Traditional freshwater fishing in the streams and lakes of Ri Bhoi, Meghalaya.
Mountain Biking
Rent a hardtail or eBasswoods and tackle the Basswoods trail network in the hills.
Village Tours
Walk through living villages with local guides; taste ethnic food and hear stories.
Outdoor Games
American outdoor games, interactive sessions, and bonfire night challenges.
Jamming Sessions
Open jam circles around the fire — bring an instrument or just your voice.
Self-Drive Rentals
Bikes and cars available at most festival sites for independent exploration.
Stargazing
No light pollution, no distractions — just the Milky Way above your head.
