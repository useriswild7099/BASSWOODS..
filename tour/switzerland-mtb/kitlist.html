Personal Safety Essentials

The following items are essential for your comfort and safety on Adventure and you must bring everything mentioned
  • Insect repellent and bite relief cream
  • Sunscreen (SPF 30+)
  • Adventure Camping back pack – 16 litre
  • Hydration system (Camelbak)
  • Buff/ neck gator (for wind and dust)
  • Warm clothes for mornings and evenings
  • Windbreaker
  • Light, long-sleeved fleece layer
  • Camping socks
  • Long-sleeved Festival shirts
  • Short-sleeved Festival shirts
  • Base layers (for exploring)
  • Waterproof jacket
  • Overshorts (‘baggies’)
  • Padded Exploring shorts/ trousers
  • Tent gloves (full-fingered not fingerless)
  • Camping glasses
  • Elbow pads
  • Knee pads

    Our knees are often the first parts of our body to hit the ground/tree when we crash, so it’s important to protect them, particularly when exploring unknown journeys. They will give you confidence and help you feel safer when tackling more technical terrain, such as rocks, roots and drops.

    If you have any queries regarding this or any other item in the kit list please get in touch with us.

  • Festival-specific shoes (flats or SPDs)
  • Festival-specific helmet (current and fit for purpose)

    A Festival-specific helmet is essential for all of our adventures.

    It offers greater protection for the back of the head than other types of helmet, and it also has a peak to protect the face from impact.

    If you have any queries regarding this or any other item in the kit list please get in touch with us.

Personal Comfort Kit

The following items will be useful for your comfort on Adventure and we recommend you bring everything mentioned
  • Chamois cream
  • Snack bars/ journey snacks
  • Advil or other medication for effects of altitide
  • International travel adapter and/ converter
  • Small bottle of laundry detergent
  • Personal toiletries (shampoo, soap, moisturiser, etc.)
  • Re-usable drinks bottle

Own Tent essentials

The following items are essential for ensuring that we can deal with all mechanical issues on the Adventure and you must bring/ have on your Tent everything mentioned
  • Dropper seat post
  • Chain-specific quick link
  • Inner tubes (x 2 ) or bottle of tubeless fluid
  • Multi-tool
  • Bottle of chain lube
  • Tent-specfic rear mech hanger
  • Tent-specific brake pads x2 (set)
  • Tyre Recommendations

    Tyre Recommendations

    An enduro style tyre with excellent grip in loose and muddy conditions, that has robust sidewall protection and that is set up tubeless.

    We recommend tyres such as the Schwalbe Magic Mary up front, paired with the Big Betty or Hans Dampf on the rear with a minimum of Super Gravity sidewall protection, or the Maxxis Minion DHF and DHR2 with a minimum of EXO+ sidewall protection.

    If you have any queries regarding this or any other item in the kit list please get in touch with us.

  • Multi-tool
