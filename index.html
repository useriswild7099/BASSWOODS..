Why Basswoods Camp

Basswoods Camp was born out of a passion for the raw beauty of Northeast India and a desire to host unforgettable adventures.

From camping at the Ziro Festival to riding ATVs in Shillong, we provide end-to-end travel, accommodation, and rental solutions. Experience the vibrant culture, music, and nature of the Northeast with our curated services and expert local crews.

Find out more
🏕 Nagaland • Hornbill Festival
Mountains & Myths of Nagaland
A carefully curated journey through tribal heritage villages and misty mountain trails around Kisama — warrior dances, traditional Naga cuisine, and pristine forest treks.
🎶 Arunachal Pradesh • Ziro Festival
All-Time Spectacular at Ziro Valley
Camp right at the heart of India's biggest indie music festival. Breathtaking rice-paddy views by day, live music under the stars by night — pure festival magic.
🌸 Meghalaya • Cherry Blossom Festival
A Dream Week in Meghalaya
Expert-guided camping in the Khasi Hills during the Cherry Blossom season — curated food trails, scenic viewpoints, and the vibrant colours of Northeast India's favourite autumn festival.
⛺ Assam • Wilderness Camp
One for the Bucket List
An immersive wilderness escape through Assam's tea gardens, river islands, and nature reserves — guided by local experts who know every hidden trail and secret viewpoint.
🌳 Cherrapunji • Living Root Trek
First Rate Cherrapunji Experience
Trek to the legendary living root bridges, explore thundering waterfalls, and camp under the clouds at the wettest place on Earth — an adventure unlike any other.
🌏 Meghalaya • Explorer Tour
Trip of a Lifetime in Meghalaya
Crystal-clear rivers, ancient root bridges, cascading waterfalls, and tribal heritage — every single day revealed a landscape more breathtaking than the last.
