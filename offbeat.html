The Map

Explore The Regions

Meghalaya

The Land of Clouds. Not just the famous root bridges, but the hidden canyons of Sohra, the crystal clear waters of Shnongpdeng, and the ancient sacred groves of Mawphlang. We take you to the edges where the tourists stop and the wilderness begins.

  • Living Root Bridges
  • Limestone Caves
  • Hidden Waterfalls
  • Village Homestays

Nagaland

The Land of Festivals and Warriors. Beyond the Hornbill Festival, explore the Dzukou Valley, the heritage villages of Kohima, and the remote tribes of Mon. Experience a culture that is as fierce as it is hospitable.

  • Dzukou Valley
  • Tribal Heritage
  • Smoked Cuisine
  • Craft Villages

Mizoram

The Land of the Hill People. Discover the blue mountains of Phawngpui, the pristine Reiek Tlang, and the rolling emerald hills that define this peaceful sanctuary. Experience the unique Mizo hospitality and the vibrant bamboo dance traditions.

  • Blue Mountains
  • Reiek Peak Trek
  • Bamboo Forest Trails
  • Traditional Crafts

Manipur

The Jewel of India. From the floating islands of Loktak Lake to the unique Keibul Lamjao National Park, Manipur offers a landscape like no other. Discover the rich heritage of Polo's birthplace and the graceful movements of the Ras Leela dance.

  • Loktak Lake
  • Floating Phumdis
  • Imphal Heritage
  • Thang-Ta Martial Arts

Sikkim

The Himalayan Gateway. Home to Kangchenjunga, the world's third highest peak, Sikkim is a land of monasteries, alpine meadows, and high-altitude lakes. Trek through the rhododendron forests or find peace in the ancient chants of Rumtek.

  • Kangchenjunga Views
  • Tsomgo Lake
  • Buddhist Monasteries
  • Yumthang Valley

Tripura

The Sacred Legacy. Discover the stunning Neermahal water palace, the rock carvings of Unakoti, and the architectural splendors of Ujjayanta Palace. A blend of royal history and deep-rooted tribal culture awaits in this quiet corner of the Northeast.

  • Neermahal Palace
  • Unakoti Carvings
  • Tribal Traditions
  • Wildlife Sanctuaries

The Wayfinder Process

We don't sell tours; we design journeys. Here is how your offbeat adventure comes to life.

01

The Dialogue

We start with a conversation. Tell us about your past travels, your physical comfort level, and the kind of "wow" moments you crave.

02

Draft Route

Our regional experts draft a logistics plan, identifying hidden trails, reliable local guides, and the best village homestays.

03

Refinement

We iterate based on your feedback. We finalize the vehicle types (4x4s for the high passes) and the specific cultural encounters.

04

Commencement

Once confirmed, we handle the permits, gear, and briefings. You arrive, we meet you, and the untracked path begins.
